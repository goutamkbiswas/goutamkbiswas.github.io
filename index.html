About

Who I Am

PhD researcher in Mechanical Engineering at the University of Central Florida, specializing in nanoscale thermal and optical phenomena in Nano-Electrospray Laser Deposition (NELD).

My work spans nanoparticle transport, laser–matter interaction, and temperature evolution in thin-film and film–substrate systems — combining advanced analytical modeling with hands-on experimental processing.

I earned my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Bangladesh University of Engineering and Technology (BUET) and am pursuing my PhD at UCF (expected Aug 2026).
